    What Do I Think Good Design Looks Like?

    First and foremost, Good Design has a clear and specific purpose - addressing a common problem that many people will relate to. That’s where every good product begins!

    Not only does it provide a welcome solution to that problem; it does so in a way that’s intuitive, engaging, and highly motivational. There are many diffrent ways to look at design.

    Web design should be simple and easy to use with a clear, straightforward interface, straightforward language, and deliberate absence of distracting visuals - making it accessible for everyone. Print design might focuses on the layout, typography, and artwork in publications - setting the tone for anything that involves the written word! Environmental design connects people to places - making them memorable or informative. This includes signage, murals, and navigation.

    Designers combine many different types of design, including programing, architectural, product, and industrial design, with graphic design to implement their work!
